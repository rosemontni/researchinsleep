# Reviewer Context For PaperReview.ai

Use this framing when preparing the upload PDF.

## What this document is

An expository reconstruction of a published elementary proof of Gleason's
theorem in dimension `3`.

## What this document is not

1. not a claim of a new theorem
2. not a claim of a wholly independent proof from scratch
3. not an ML paper

## Desired review focus

Please focus feedback on:

1. clarity of exposition
2. honesty of mathematical claim level
3. whether the proof architecture is understandable
4. whether any steps still appear overcompressed or misleading

## Important limitation of the reviewer system

PaperReview.ai is grounded in recent arXiv literature and is likely strongest
for AI papers. This submission is mathematics/exposition, so structural and
clarity feedback is more important than domain-confidence on fine proof details.
