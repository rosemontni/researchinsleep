# Venue Scorecard

Use this scorecard to rank the longlist before selecting the final three.

## Scoring Axes

Score each journal on a 1 to 5 scale.

- Scope fit
  - Does the journal regularly publish work on this topic and contribution type?
- Prestige
  - How selective, visible, and respected is the venue within the field?
- Reachability
  - Is the current paper realistically strong enough for this journal?
- Evidence fit
  - Do the paper's experiments, scale, and validation style match what the journal usually accepts?
- Presentation fit
  - Can the current manuscript be adapted to the journal's style without distorting the work?
- Practical fit
  - Are page limits, article type, review times, and submission constraints acceptable?

## Reachability Heuristic

Do not confuse prestige with reachability.

Ask:

- Is the paper benchmark-grade or still prototype-grade?
- Are the claims narrower than the evidence, or still too ambitious?
- Does the work have the breadth expected by broad-scope elite venues?
- Does the paper look like something this journal already publishes?

## Output Format

For each longlist venue, capture:

- Journal name
- Publisher
- Official scope link
- 3 to 5 recent relevant papers
- Score summary
- Key upside
- Main risk
- Decision: keep, backup, or reject
